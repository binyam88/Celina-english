About Us

Celina English is a premier language academy dedicated to helping students master English through interactive and fun methods. Founded in Addis Ababa, we serve beginners to advanced learners. We provide personalized language classes to help you develop your language skills for personal and professional success. Our experienced coaches will work with you to design a program that meets your specific needs and goals. At our language coaching academy, we are committed to delivering exceptional service and helping you achieve your language-related objectives.
